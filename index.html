Project Plan - Software Discovery Dashboard

  • Software Methodology: Scrum
    • Our project makes heavy use of a metadata standard that is still in active development. We will be collaborating with the team making the standard along the way, and may have to alter our implementation based on additions/deletions to their project. Because of this, we decided to use the Scrum methodology. Scrum lends itself to frequent iterations and the ability to respond quickly to feedback.
  • Process
    • Meet with interested parties: Sponsor, Other organizations, previous contributors
      • utilize video conferencing (Skype, Google Hangouts)
    • 4-up charts
  • Spring Outline
    • Four-week sprints, Tuesday to Tuesday
    • Iteration planning and retrospective - Monday before the start of a new iteration
      • Planning poker will be used for estimating stories
    • Demo - Thursday after the end of a sprint
    • Daily stand-up meetings utilizing Slack
  • Sprint 0
    • Become with familiar prior research
    • Meet with other people involved
    • Discuss expectations and requirements
  • Sprint 1
    • To be decided
